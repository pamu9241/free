Kylie Resort

With a focus on the tourism business, Kylie Group opted to open a Kylie Grand Luxury branch in the southern city of Galle, which is one of Sri Lanka's most popular tourist attractions. The Kylie Galle branch has made a significant effort to deliver funds to Sri Lanka. Kylie is dedicated to delivering all amenities to all domestic and foreign guests. It also features a tourist spot where visitors can see the entire city of Galle.
